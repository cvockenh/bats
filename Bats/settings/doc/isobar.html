Isobar correction

A correction of the counts in the gate of the radioisotope can be done depending on some other measured sample depending measured properties, if needed (not necessary a real isobar). In Corrections > Isobar correction the isobar corrected ratios of blank samples are plotted against a possible interference.

For example for radiocarbon (Micadas system) you can do a correction for 13C molecule fragments that can get into the 14C-gate. The molecule fragments are measured as a current in a cup and from this a correction on the counts of 14C in the detector can be done.
